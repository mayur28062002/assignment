"NASA Kepler Exoplanet Search Results": This dataset contains information about potential exoplanets (planets outside our solar system) discovered by NASA's Kepler spacecraft. It includes various attributes such as planetary characteristics, orbital parameters, and detection methods. It's a fascinating dataset for exploring the field of astronomy and exoplanet research.
